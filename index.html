General Info

Satoru Iwata was perhaps one of the most iconic and memorable figures in the video game industry. From his humble beginnings with making video games on a programmable calculator to becoming Nintendo’s 4th president, his technical genius and pure passion for video games led him to become a leading figure in the industry. His work brought the Nintendo Wii and Nintendo DS into the gaming world, in an effort to, as he said himself, “expand the gaming population.” Iwata deeply believed that games should be accessible to everyone, regardless of age, gender and experience. His contributions to the industry, valuable insights, exceptional leadership and positive and charismatic personality make him an unforgettable presence in the industry; loved by gamers and developers worldwide.

On my business card, I am a corporate president. In my mind, I am a game developer. But in my heart, I am a gamer. - Satoru Iwata
