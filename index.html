General Info

Welcome to the Satoru Iwata Tribute Website. As the name suggests, this website was created as a tribute to Satoru Iwata. He was a leading figure in the video game industry and left a lasting legacy on Nintendo, shaping what it is today. The purpose of this website is to recognize and respect Satoru Iwata's impact on the industry, delivering such information directly to you.
